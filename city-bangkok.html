Bangkok: The Smart Capital of ASEAN

üö§ Smart Mobility

Electric Ferries & Smart Piers

Bangkok has modernized its famous river transport:
1. E-Boats: Fleet of air-conditioned, aluminum electric boats on Chao Phraya.
2. Smart Piers: Digital screens showing real-time boat arrival times.
3. Cashless Ticketing: Payment via Rabbit Card or banking apps.
4. Noise Reduction: Silent engines reducing noise pollution on the river.
GOAL: Modernizing the "Venice of the East" with green tech.

üì° 5G Connectivity

ASEAN's 5G Leader

Bangkok was one of the first cities in SE Asia to launch 5G:
1. Bang Sue Grand Station: The largest station in ASEAN is a 5G smart hub.
2. Smart Hospitals: Siriraj Hospital uses 5G for AI diagnosis and cloud data.
3. Coverage: 99% of Bangkok is covered by high-speed 5G networks.
4. IoT Industry: Factories using private 5G networks for automation.
GOAL: Driving the digital economy of Southeast Asia.

üí∏ FinTech & Payments

PromptPay & Cashless Society

Thailand has one of the world's best real-time payment systems:
1. PromptPay: Transfer money instantly using just a phone number.
2. QR Code Standard: Pay street food vendors or malls via a single QR code.
3. Cross-Border: QR payments now work in Singapore, Japan, and Vietnam.
4. Digital Wallet: Government distributing digital money via blockchain wallets.
GOAL: A fully cashless economy accessible to everyone.

Green Bangkok 2030

Activities & Events in Bangkok

The Grand Palace

Explore the spectacular former residence of the Kings and the sacred Temple of the Emerald Buddha.

Wat Arun (Dawn Temple)

Visit the stunning riverside temple known for its colorful spires, best viewed at sunset.

Floating Markets

Take a boat ride at Damnoen Saduak to buy fruits, food, and souvenirs directly from boats.

Street Food Tour

Taste world-famous street food in Chinatown (Yaowarat) and try Pad Thai and Mango Sticky Rice.

Chatuchak Market

Shop at the world's largest weekend market with over 15,000 stalls selling clothes, art, and pets.

Khao San Road

Experience the backpacker hub famous for its vibrant nightlife, bars, music, and street parties.

Traditional Thai Massage

Relax with an authentic Thai massage at Wat Pho or luxury spas like Health Land.

Chao Phraya Dinner Cruise

Enjoy a buffet dinner on a cruise ship while watching the illuminated temples along the river.

IconSiam Mall

Visit the luxury riverside mall featuring an indoor floating market and a multimedia water show.

Lumphini Park

Escape the city noise, ride swan boats, and spot monitor lizards in this massive green park.

Rooftop Bars

Sip cocktails with a skyline view at famous rooftop bars like Sky Bar (from The Hangover movie).
