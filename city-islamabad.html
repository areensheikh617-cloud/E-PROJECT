About Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan, known for its modern infrastructure, wide roads, and greenery. It is a planned city located at the foot of the Margalla Hills, offering a clean and organized environment compared to other major Pakistani cities.

Safe City Project & AI Security

Islamabad is the safest city in Pakistan due to AI integration:
1. Over 2,500 smart cameras with facial recognition installed.
2. Automated E-Challan system for traffic violations.
3. AI software detects suspicious bags or activities automatically.
4. 4G-connected patrol cars with live monitoring.
GOAL: To make Islamabad a crime-free and secure capital.

Islamabad Technopolis (STZ)

Government has launched a massive Special Technology Zone (STZ):
1. Tax-free zone for international tech companies.
2. High-speed fiber optic internet for all offices.
3. Home to global giants like Samsung and Huawei offices.
4. Creating 50,000+ jobs for software engineers.
GOAL: To turn Islamabad into the "Silicon Valley" of Pakistan.

Islamabad City App Services

Citizens can now control civic services from their phone:
1. Pay property taxes and water bills online.

Activities & Events in Islamabad

Hiking & Nature Trails

Hiking on Trail 3 & 5, walking in Margalla Hills National Park, and sunset views at Daman-e-Koh.

Heritage & Landmarks

Visit the iconic Faisal Mosque, Pakistan Monument, and the historic Saidpur Village.

Dining with a View

Dinner at The Monal & La Montana (Pir Sohawa), and diverse cuisines at F-7 & F-6 Markaz.

Parks & Recreation

Boating at Rawal Lake (Lake View Park), F-9 Fatima Jinnah Park, and Shakarparian Hills.

Shopping & Malls

The Centaurus Mall, Safa Gold Mall, Giga Mall (DHA), and traditional shopping at Jinnah Super.

Lok Mela (Folk Festival)

Annual folk festival at Lok Virsa Museum featuring artisans, music, and food from all provinces.

Arts & Literature

Islamabad Literature Festival (ILF), PNCA art exhibitions, and theater plays.

Sports & Adventure

Cricket at Shalimar Ground, Paragliding at Khanpur Dam (nearby), and Islamabad Sports Complex.

Weekly Bazaars

The famous Sunday Bazaar (H-9) for thrifting, antiques, organic vegetables, and cheap clothing.

Gaming & Bowling

Bowling at Megazone (F-9 Park), F1 Traxx for go-karting, and gaming arcades in Safa Gold Mall.

Cafe Culture & Chai

Enjoying evening tea at 'Chaye Khana', Quetta Tea spots, and trendy cafes in F-11 and E-7.
