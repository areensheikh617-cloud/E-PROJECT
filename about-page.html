DNBC News (Digital News Broadcasting Corporation) was founded in 2020 with a vision to provide authentic, unbiased, and timely news coverage to audiences across Pakistan and beyond. We are committed to journalistic excellence and maintaining the highest standards of integrity in reporting.

Over the years, DNBC has grown from a small startup to one of Pakistan's most trusted news organizations, with millions of readers and viewers relying on us for accurate information about local and international events.

Our team of experienced journalists, reporters, and editors work around the clock to bring you comprehensive coverage of politics, business, sports, entertainment, technology, and more. We believe in the power of informed citizens and strive to empower our audience with knowledge.
